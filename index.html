Food:

The Traveller's Rest sells a wide selection of drinks and homemade food.

The kitchen boasts 4 star reviews on TripAdvisor and serves traditional pub fare, including dishes such as lasagne, steak & ale pies, and beer-battered fish & chips.

There will also be plenty of snacks available.

For the festival there will also be a woodfire pizza oven, and a BBQ outside.

We have vegetarian and vegan options and have options for many dietary restrictions. If you have any questions concerns please talk to a member of staff.
